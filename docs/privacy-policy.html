StickerSwapbook Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

StickerSwapbook is a sticker collection tracker and swap helper. This policy explains how the app handles data.

StickerSwapbook is an independent app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any tournament organizer, sports federation, sticker publisher, or sticker brand.

Data We Store

StickerSwapbook stores your sticker collection on your device. This includes sticker codes, quantities, missing stickers, and duplicate stickers. This data is stored locally using device storage.

Data We Collect

StickerSwapbook does not collect personal information, create user accounts, use analytics, show ads, or send your sticker collection to our servers.

Sharing

If you choose to share your swap list, StickerSwapbook creates a text summary of your missing and duplicate stickers and passes it to your device's share sheet. You choose which app receives that text. Those third-party apps handle the shared text according to their own policies.

Importing Swap Lists

If you paste or import a swap list from another person, the app uses that text on your device to compare sticker matches. The imported text is not sent to us.

Retention and Deletion

Sticker data remains on your device until you reset it in the app, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. Because we do not operate accounts or servers for this app, we do not hold a remote copy of your sticker data.

Security

The app keeps sticker collection data on your device. We do not transmit this data to our own servers. Any data you choose to share is handled by the app you select from your device's share sheet.

Children

StickerSwapbook is a general sticker collection utility. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy if the app's data practices change. The updated policy will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact email listed on the StickerSwapbook Google Play listing or submit an inquiry at github.com/NaumZ13/swapbook-sticker-collectors/issues .
